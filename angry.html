Feelin' angry? Let's cool down.

Let's take some steps to chill out.

  1. Take a deep breathe. Focus on your breathing.
  2. If possible, remove yourself from the situation.
  3. Practice boundary setting and enforce those boundaries.
  4. If you're experiencing fight or flight symptoms, do a physical activity to bring yourself down. For example, jumping jacks or running in place.
